Felix' Workshop

Small self-serve tools, built one at a time and left running. No accounts, no uploads that don't come back to you.

Draw SVG

Live

Set a pixel-sized canvas, draw freehand in any color, and export exactly what you drew as a real SVG file.

PDF Toolkit

Coming soon

Merge, split, reorder, and compress PDFs, remove or extract pages, add page numbers, convert scanned pages to searchable text, and export pages as images — everything the average PDF site does, plus a "make this acceptable for upload" mode that fits a file to a target size and format. Runs fully client-side: your documents never leave your browser.

Metadata Cleaner

Coming soon

See exactly what's hiding in a file — camera EXIF and GPS in photos, author and edit history in PDFs, tracked changes in office documents — then strip it in one click. Works on images, PDFs, and most common document formats.

Image Toolkit

Coming soon

Convert between PNG/JPEG/WebP/AVIF/HEIC, remove backgrounds, resize or pad without distorting, batch-rename, and generate favicons, app-icon sets, and social-media sizes from one source image. Also home to the pixel randomizer: every color gets swapped for a new, unrelated random one, consistently, so identical pixels always match but neighbors can end up worlds apart.

SVG Toolkit

Coming soon

Upload an existing SVG to keep editing it in Draw SVG, or reach for the deeper tools here: optimize and simplify paths, replace colors, convert strokes and text to paths, normalize the viewBox, and build sprites from a folder of icons. The flagship: "Fix my SVG," which auto-resolves clipping, missing viewBoxes, and oversized canvases in one pass.

QR Code & Barcodes

Coming soon

Generate QR codes styled to match this site instead of plain black squares, still verified to scan cleanly, plus Wi-Fi, contact-card, and calendar-event QR codes, barcode generation and reading, and batch generation from a CSV for print-ready label sheets.

Random Number Generator

Coming soon

Draw random numbers from uniform, normal, exponential, binomial, Poisson, and other distributions, in up to ten dimensions at once, with an optional seed for reproducible runs. Export the results as a table, CSV, or JSON.

Audio Toolkit

Coming soon

Convert between MP3/WAV/M4A/OGG, trim, join, and normalize volume, remove silence, extract audio from video, and compress to a target file size. Also generates waveform images and strips audio metadata.

Video Toolkit

Coming soon

Trim without re-encoding, strip audio, extract a frame, convert to and from GIF, compress to a target size, and fix aspect ratio or orientation. Plus subtitle conversion and timing shifts, and loop/boomerang clip generation.

Text & Document Utilities

Coming soon

Clean up pasted text: dedupe and sort lines, fix smart quotes and invisible Unicode characters, diff two files, and convert between Markdown, HTML, and plain text. Also formats and minifies JSON, YAML, CSS, and SQL, and counts tokens for common AI models.

CSV & Spreadsheet Toolkit

Coming soon

"Fix this CSV" detects encoding, delimiter, and broken rows automatically, plus merge, split, dedupe, and diff CSV files, convert to and from XLSX and JSON, reshape wide/long data, and pull a random sample of rows.

Developer Tools

Coming soon

JSON/JWT/Base64/URL formatting and decoding, UUID and hash generation, a regex tester, cron explainer, and converters for cURL, TypeScript types, and Zod schemas — the everyday grab-bag of things a browser tab full of separate sites usually handles.

Font Tools

Coming soon

Convert TTF/OTF to WOFF2, preview every glyph, detect missing characters, subset a font down to the characters you actually use, and generate a ready-to-paste @font-face block.

Color & Design Tools

Coming soon

Convert between HEX/RGB/HSL/OKLCH, check and auto-repair contrast, extract a palette from an image, build tint/shade scales, and preview a palette under different types of color-vision deficiency.

Archive & File Tools

Coming soon

Zip and unzip files entirely in-browser, strip macOS junk files from an archive, find duplicates by hash, generate and verify checksums, and sanitize or bulk-rename a folder of filenames.

Geospatial Tools

Coming soon

Convert between GPX, KML, and GeoJSON, extract or remove GPS data from photos, calculate distances, and simplify or merge GPX tracks.

Calendar & Date Tools

Coming soon

View and convert ICS files, merge calendars, shift event times, fix timezone mistakes, and generate a recurring-event ICS from a plain-language rule.

Email Tools

Coming soon

View EML files and their attachments, inspect headers with a plain-language SPF/DKIM/DMARC explanation, preview HTML emails at different widths, and strip tracking pixels from saved HTML.

Odds & Ends

Coming soon

Every tool in this workshop runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you upload, draw, or edit is ever sent to a server, and this workshop is open source, so you don't have to take my word for it.
